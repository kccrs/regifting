How to Use Our Service

1. Enter Your Login Info

You will need to enter in your name, email address, mailing address, payment information, and the worst gift you have ever received. Aside from the latter, this process should be familiar to you. We ask for your worst ever gift because we enter all new customers in a monthly contest in which the worst gift wins six months free service.

2. Choose your subscription

Are there black sheep who don't appreciate your "presence" at family gatherings? If you'd like to see who is regifting the fruits of your hard-earned cash, you'll need to part with just a little of it now. Check out our three-tiered plans: Bad Santa, Max the Grinch Dog, or our platinum option, The Gift that Keeps on Giving.

3. Choose your Poison: Stickers or Amazon?

We offer two services: mail-order barcodes to track all your thoughtful but unappreciated gifts, and an Amazon integration where you can enter your information on Amazon. We offer a discount for purchasers of both services and also offer an option to sign in with Amazon.
